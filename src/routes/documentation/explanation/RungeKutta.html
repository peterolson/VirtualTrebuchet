The trebuchet simulation uses the fifth order Runge-Kutta method to solve the Equations of Motion. It also uses the fourth order Runge-Kutta method to check the accuracy of the solution. Any numerical method only gives an approximation of the actual answer, and its accuracy is dependent on the step size, h. The way the simulation determines it is using a small enough step size is that it compares the answers of the fourth and fifth order methods to make sure the answers agree within a specified tolerance of each other.
